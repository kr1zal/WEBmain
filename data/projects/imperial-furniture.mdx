## Overview

**Role:** E-commerce Director / IT Director
**Duration:** Jan 2020 — June 2025 (5 years 6 months)

Built the entire e-commerce ecosystem from scratch for a major furniture manufacturer. Transformed a traditional offline business into a digital-first enterprise.

## Key Achievements

*   **Revenue Growth:** Scaled online revenue from **300K to 26M ₽/mo** (87x growth) in 4 years.
*   **Profitability:** Maintained **19-20% operating margin** with full P&L responsibility.
*   **Team Building:** Built 8 departments from scratch, managing up to **60 specialists**.
*   **Automation:** Automated **70% of business processes** using n8n, Make, and AI agents.

## Technical Implementation

### Infrastructure & Architecture
Designed a microservices architecture using an API-first approach.
*   **Stack:** Yandex Cloud, PostgreSQL, Redis.
*   **Integrations:** 15+ REST API integrations (ERP, CRM, Marketplaces, Payment Gateways).
*   **Resilience:** Architecture handled 87x load growth without code rewriting.
*   **Monitoring:** Reduced incident response time from 2 hours to 5 minutes.

### Automation & AI
Orchestrated complex workflows to replace manual labor.
*   **n8n Workflows:** 25+ active workflows automating order processing, logistics, and customer service.
*   **AI Agents:** Deployed GPT/Claude agents for content generation and demand forecasting.
*   **Results:** Reduced warehouse overstock by **25%** through AI-driven demand forecasting.

## Business Strategy

The transformation was executed in phases:
1.  **Digital Marketing (2020):** Launched multi-channel acquisition (PPC, SEO), achieving 350%+ ROI.
2.  **Infrastructure (2020-2025):** Built internal IT department and proprietary software.
3.  **Marketplaces (2022-2023):** Expanded to Ozon and Wildberries, generating 45M+ ₽/mo combined revenue.
4.  **Logistics:** Standardized packaging for rFBS models, reducing returns and damages.
