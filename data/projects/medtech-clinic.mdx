## Overview

**Role:** E-commerce Director
**Duration:** 2021 — 2023 (2 years)

Launched and scaled the online division for a private medical clinic, focusing on patient retention (LTV) and digital service delivery.

## Key Achievements

*   **LTV Growth:** Increased patient Lifetime Value from **3K to 27-32K ₽/year** (9x growth).
*   **Revenue:** Doubled monthly revenue from 3M to 6M ₽.
*   **Cost Efficiency:** Reduced Cost Per Lead (CPL) by **30%** through channel optimization.
*   **Operating Margin:** Achieved 18% profitability.

## Strategy & Execution

### Building the Structure
Established 5 new departments from the ground up:
*   Call Center (Process standardization, scripts, KPI)
*   Marketing (Web + Digital)
*   IT Infrastructure
*   SMM & Community Management
*   Service & Quality Control

### Digital Ecosystem
*   **CRM & Telephony:** Integrated amoCRM with UIS telephony for seamless patient tracking.
*   **Analytics:** Implemented end-to-end analytics (Roistat, GA4) to track patient journey.
*   **Marketing Mix:** Combined performance marketing (Google/Yandex Ads) with Influence Marketing and PR.

## Compliance & Security
Ensured full compliance with **152-FZ** (Personal Data Protection Law) regarding patient data storage and processing.
